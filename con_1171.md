## Details
 **Description:** The people of Jianyang city in the province of Sichuan prepare this dish by cooking mutton in a stock boiled from carp and mutton bones. As a Chinese saying goes, the combination of fish and mutton is bound to taste fresh, although this particular pot of food seems to be insufficiently heated.
