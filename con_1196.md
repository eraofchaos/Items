## Details
 **Description:** The Chongqing Hotpot is an important cultural symbol of Chongqing. To make it, beef tripe, pork aorta, and duck intestines are boiled in a mala soup base. The dish has a refreshingly delectable taste, although this particular pot of food still seems rather raw.
